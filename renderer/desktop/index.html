Scan To Connect

Scan this QR code with your phone camera to open the VibraLink receiver page instantly.

Instructions

  1. Keep this desktop app open.
  2. Select the Windows output device or a virtual cable for capture.
  3. Choose a quality mode, then click Start Streaming.
  4. Open the displayed URL on your phone over the same WiFi.
  5. Tap Connect on the phone page.
